Sponsoring conferences will get you programmers who want large paychecks.

Sending speakers to conferences will get you programmers who want to work on cool systems.

Delve into all of this and how you need both and you need to determine what you're looking for. And either is OK. You just have to know the strengths and weaknesses of both sides.
